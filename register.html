QUIZZITCH

From Mr. Darcy to Begum Jaan, we have it all here. Showcase your knowledge by taking a deep dive into the world of literature. Welcome to "Quizzitch", the Literature Quiz organised as a part of the Presidency University Literature Festival 1.0, by the century-old Presidency College Society for Acquisition of General Knowledge (PCSAGK).

Rules and Regulations:
  • 1. The quiz is restricted to college/university/institute students only.
  • 2 There will be a total of two rounds for this event.
  • a. Prelims Round which will be conducted in written format. The best 6 teams shall advance to the finals. In case of a tiebreaker, starred questions will be asked.
  • b. Finals - The six teams shall compete against each other by answering a total of 30 questions. There will be a provision for infinite pounce and bounce in this round.
  • 3. Each team shall consist of a maximum of two members. Cross-college teams are allowed as well.
  • 4. Reporting Time - 1.30 p.m.

ANECDOTE

Short Film

We welcome all the enthusiastic emerging filmmakers who would like to tell a story through the lens of their camera. Be it from the core of your personal experiences or the commentary you've been carrying in your mind, Presidency University welcomes you to join in the Short Film competition as part of The Presidency Literary Festival 1.0 organised by the Presidency University Film Society (PUFS).

Rules and Regulations:

1. The maximum duration of the short film must not exceed 10 minutes, including opening and closing credits.
2. The short film must bear English subtitles.
3. The short film must not have been publicly screened or released before the festival.
4. The short film must not contain any copyrighted material unless the makers have adequate
permission/certification to use it.
5. The short film must not contain any offensive or inappropriate content, including but not limited to excessive violence, hate speech, and discrimination of any kind.
6. The short film must be submitted in mp4 or MP4 format. via a secure, open cloud file link (eg. iCloud, Google Drive, etc.), to be attached to the email body.
7. Each participant can submit only one entry.
8. The participant must be the rightful owner of the submitted film and have all necessary permissions for the use of the film's content.
9. The festival organisers and the jury reserve the right to disqualify any entry that does not meet the contest rules or standards.
10. The top three short films will be screened on the final day of the festival - 31st March, 2023, and the winners will be announced thereafter.
11. The subject line should be "Submission for Anecdote - TPLF 1.0". The participants also have to mention their Name, Batch/Year of Study, Affiliating Institution, Contact number and Email Address in the mail body. Lack of any of these details will lead to disqualification.

Registration fees:

Rs. 30

GAME OVER

Character Face-off

What happens when two of your favourite fictional characters come alive and converse on topics of utmost interest? What happens when you are asked to step in their shoes and enact the arguments for them? Game Over - the character face-off competition anticipates such intriguing debates ranging from existentialism, accountability and desperation, stretching as far as the imagination goes. The event aims at a synaesthetic experience performed on stage, freed from the boundaries of virtuality and textuality.

Rules and Regulations:

1. Participants are required to pair up and make teams of two for the event and enact out debates concerning the allotted topics through the point of view of the chosen, personified fictional character- sets.
2. The allotted time slot for each team is 6 minutes. A warning bell would be rung after the first 5 minutes are over.
3. Participants can resort to English, Bengali or Hindi during the face-off. Switching between languages is also permitted.
4. Participants are encouraged to come up with original, interactive arguments which would reflect their sound knowledge of the chosen characters and the topic of concern- based on which they would be judged.

REDISCOVER

Cover Illustration

Remember how you always used to doodle images and sequences from your favourite book at the back of your math notebook? What if years later you were told that the doodles you used to keep hidden away, have finally been given a chance to come to life? The Presidency Literature Festival 1.0 brings to you "RedisCover", an exciting opportunity for you to imagine and re-imagine the cover of your favourite book, in whatever medium you want. Conjure up all those emotions of pain, anger, love and elation you felt while reading and re-reading your favourite literary masterpiece, digitally or on a page, and weave your tale through this fun competition of cover illustration.

Rules and Regulations:

1. Participants can choose any book of their choice written in Bengali, English or Hindi.
2. Each participant is allowed only one entry.
3. Every submission must be an original work - digital art is permitted too. However, plagiarising from any existing illustration of any edition of the book will lead to disqualification.
4. The format for submission should be .png/.jpg and the image orientation should be portrait. The name of the file should be "Full name of the participant_RedisCover"
5. The subject line should be "Submission for RedisCover - TPLF 1.0". The participants have to mention their Name, Batch/Year of Study, Affiliating Institution, Contact number and Email Address in the mail body. Lack of any of these details will lead to disqualification.
